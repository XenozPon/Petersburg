sights of St. Petersburg

1.State Hermitage Museum - Russian State Art and Cultural-Historical Museum in St. Petersburg, one of the largest institutions of its kind in the world.

2.Saint Isaac's Cathedral - the largest Orthodox church in St. Petersburg. Located on St. Isaac's Square. Cathedral of the St. Petersburg diocese from 1858 to 1929. Since 1928 it has the status of a museum.

3.Palace Square - the main square of St. Petersburg, an architectural ensemble that arose in the second half of the 18th - first half of the 19th century.

4.Winter Palace - the main imperial palace of Russia, located on Palace Square in St. Petersburg.
